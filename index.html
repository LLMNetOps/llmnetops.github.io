About LLMNetOps

Bridging the AI Literacy Gap in Network Operations

Many IDREN member institutions struggle to adopt AI-supported network management due to limited staff, uneven expertise, and reliance on undocumented knowledge. Generative AI offers potential for improving documentation and troubleshooting, but most administrators lack training, confidence, and tools that respect data privacy‚Äîcreating an AI-literacy gap.


LLMNetOps aims to address this gap by building foundational AI knowledge, fostering peer collaboration, and promoting locally-hosted open-source LLM models as privacy-aware solutions tailored to institutional needs in network operations.
